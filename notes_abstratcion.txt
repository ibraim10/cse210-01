What is the definition of abstraction?
is the process of turning complex ideas into simple ones. It is removing characteristics from something so that only the essential ones remain.
What is an object?
is a conceptual model for a category of things, real or imagined, that has a specific responsibility within our program. For example, we might think of an object that holds and provides identifying information about a person.
